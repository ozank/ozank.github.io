#### How to request an appointment

1. **Check my schedule above** and find a suitable slot.

2. **Create the event in your own calendar** and send me an invitation (.ics file) — see the guides for [Gmail](https://support.google.com/calendar/answer/37161?hl=en-GB&ctx=tltp&rd=1) or [Outlook](http://smallbusiness.chron.com/send-invitation-outlook-calendar-gmail-account-74988.html).

3. **Wait for confirmation.** If I'm available, I'll accept and the meeting is added to my calendar automatically. If not, I'll propose an alternative time.
